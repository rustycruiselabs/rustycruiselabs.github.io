Rolling Mind

Overview

“Rolling Mind” is a mobile puzzle game designed to push the boundaries of your spatial cognition. Developed with insights from the National Institutes of Health, it focuses on mental rotation, a critical component of spatial thinking. Mental rotation entails envisioning objects from different angles in the mind’s eye. In “Rolling Mind,” players are tasked with navigating 3D objects along specified paths, challenging their capacity to visualize and manipulate spatial configurations.
