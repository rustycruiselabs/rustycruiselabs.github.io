Blob Bridge

Overview

Blob Bridge was my submission for the Gamedev.js Game Jam 2025. It started as a small, relaxed project—a break from more intense development work. Out of 416 entries, the game ranked 158th, earning a 3.4/5 rating for gameplay, which I’m quite happy with, especially since gameplay was always my main priority.

That said, due to time constraints, the gameplay wasn’t fully realized during the jam. I’m now planning to release a complete mobile version of Blob Bridge to fully bring the idea to life—stay tuned!
