Rough Track

Overview

RoughTrack is a lightweight, opinionated tool designed to help teams plan and communicate priorities through simple Now-Next-Later roadmaps. It focuses on clarity over complexity, allowing teams to capture what matters most, stay aligned, and share progress effortlessly.

It’s not built to be the next Jira or Linear. RoughTrack is a simplified tool for small teams and individuals who just want a straightforward way to prioritize tasks and communicate what’s coming next.

To use this, simply self host it yourself! Guide can be found in Github.
