Renji

Overview

Renji is a personal relationship journal that helps you remember the people you have interacted with. Whether they are clients at work, colleagues, friends, or important connections, Renji lets you capture notes, events, and to-dos for each person, with timely reminders to help you stay on top of follow-ups, even when life gets busy.

With Renji, you can quickly log notes, set reminders, and keep track of all your interactions in life. Renji acts as your personal assistant, helping you stay on top of to-dos and upcoming follow-ups, with daily summary check-ins to keep everything in context.

Check out the features Renji has below!

FAQ

Is it free?
Yes. Renji is free to use, with optional premium features such as calendar sync, grouped reminders, and next-action suggestions in daily notifications.
Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device and stays with you. Renji does not expose or share your personal notes.

In future versions, we may add optional encrypted cloud sync to support multi-device access.

Do I need to add everything manually?
You have to add notes/events/todos for each persona manually, but Renji is designed to make it quick and lightweight so it doesn’t feel like extra work.
Can I use Renji offline?
Yes. You can view and update your journal even without an internet connection. Your data is stored locally on your device.
Will I get reminders to follow up?

Yes. You can set reminders for events and to-dos so you don’t forget to follow up at the right time.

On the free plan, you receive a daily notification at 8am. On the pro plan, you can choose when to receive your reminders.
