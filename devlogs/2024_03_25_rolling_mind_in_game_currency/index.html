Rolling Mind - In Game Currency

Introducing Coins

“Coins” - an in-game currency are introduced to allow players to purchase hints or unlock new model skins. The goal is to provide players with a challenge where

  1. Hints cannot be used easily due to limited resources (coins)
  2. New model skins unlocked varieties in the gameplay.

Earning Coins

So how can players earn coins?

  1. In App Purchase

    Coin bundles are common game monetization strategies which I won’t go too much into details.

  2. Player Rewards

    To reward players for playing our game, I think it is fair that there are ways for players to earn coins without having to spend a dime.

    • Clearing Puzzles

      Clearing puzzles are the easiest way to earn coins. There are a couple of interesting ways to make clearing puzzle rewards fun.

      1. Chance Based Rewards - an example is allowing players to choose 1 out of 3 chest box where each box have different reward amount.

      2. Objective Based Rewards - an example is where players clear the puzzle without using hints, or players beat the best time.

    • Daily Logins

      Lastly, daily logins is a useful feature to encourage players to come back and play the game everyday to challenge themselves! I have yet to come up with this feature yet! So look forward to future devlogs!

    • Hourly Free Gifts

      This is the hourly free gift feature that was created. There were 2 attempts, but I like the latter one as it looks cleaner on the UI.
