Rolling Mind - Monetization Strategy

Intro

As it is closer to my release date, it is time to think about the monetization strategy for the game. Although this is a hobby, I still hope to be able to earn some side income from my work & efforts. For my first mobile game attempt, I think that a freemium model with rewarded ads and in app purchases will suit “Rolling Mind” since it is a puzzle genre game.

The tricky part for me is how to balance the game such that even without using rewarded ads or in app purchases, the game is still playable.

How are In Game Currency Used?

Firstly, building on the previous devlog on In Game Currency, players can use coins for the following actions:

  1. Use Hints

  2. Unlock new model skins

It is worth noting that there are limited amount of skins to keep the game interesting. Hence, it is not very viable to use model skins as a non-consumable item for in-app purchases.

Implementing Monetization

So let’s talk about how monetization can be implemented. Given the limited options for players to spend coins, there wasn’t really an incentive for players to earn coins. Hence, I decided to keep the amount of coins earned through normal game play low, but at an amount that still allows players to enjoy the game without rewarded ads or in-app purchases.

Rewarded Advertisements

Personally, I hate banner ads so I want to focus on making use of rewarded ads to give my players a choice when it comes to showing ads. Here are some areas where rewarded ads are used.

1. Clear Game Bonus

After each puzzle completion, players will earn coins and is given an option to earn additional coins by watching advertisements.

2. Daily Free Coins

For players who do not want to spend money buying coins, they can simply watch advertisements to earn coins which is available on a daily basis.

In App Purchase

For In App Purchases (IAP), due to the game design, there wasn’t much items that could be sold. Below are some which I thought would be useful for players who want an advantage.

1. Consumable Items - Coins

Purchasing coin bundles provide players with advantages to spend on more hints and also unlocking skins to increase variety in the puzzles.

2. Consumable Items - Boosters

Boosters deals with time limits allows players to enjoy the puzzle at their own pace with some advantages.
